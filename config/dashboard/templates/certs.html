Certificates Console Commands

The stack provides several Makefile commands to manage, inspect, and clean SSL/TLS certificates directly from the console:

  • make certs-info
    Analyze Certificates (Summary): Compares active certificates in acme.json against the domains listed in domains.csv and prints a quick status summary.
  • make certs-inspect
    Detailed Inspection: Performs a comprehensive comparison of certificates against domains.csv, listing all covered domains, SANs, status, and expiration dates.
  • make certs-watch
    Monitor ACME Logs: Streams and filters Traefik container logs in real-time, focusing on ACME validation stages, challenge resolutions, and certification errors.
  • make certs-prune
    Prune Unused (Dry-Run): Identifies and lists certificates inside acme.json that belong to domains no longer defined in domains.csv. Does not modify the file.
  • make certs-prune-force
    Force Prune (Destructive): Permanently removes old/unused certificates from acme.json and hot-reloads Traefik to apply changes. Creates a backup automatically.
